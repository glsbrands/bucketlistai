60 Adventure Bucket List Ideas
That Actually Deliver

12 min read March 2026 Adventure

Most adventure bucket lists are the same fifteen things. We went further. These 60 ideas range from the accessible to the genuinely extraordinary — organized by what kind of adventurer you are right now, not the one you're pretending to be.

Some of these will scare you. That's intentional. The experiences that cost you the most courage tend to return the most.

In the Air
Leave the Ground Behind
01
Skydive Tandem Over a Spectacular Landscape
New Zealand's South Island, Hawaii, the Swiss Alps. 60 seconds of freefall changes your relationship with fear permanently.
02
Paraglide in the Alps or Himalayas
Launch from a ridge and soar for 30 minutes above a mountain landscape. Quieter than skydiving. Equally unforgettable.
03
Hot Air Balloon Over Cappadocia at Sunrise
The fairy chimneys of Turkey at dawn from 1,000 feet up. One of the most photographed experiences on earth — because it earns it.
04
Wingsuit Flying (With Proper Training)
Not for beginners. But for those who want to know what it feels like to genuinely fly — this is the answer.
05
Take a Small Plane Flight Over the Grand Canyon
The scale of the canyon only makes sense from above. A 30-minute flight will recalibrate your sense of size.
06
Hang Glide
Tandem hang gliding is available in dozens of destinations. The silent soaring is unlike anything motorized.
On the Water
Into the Deep
07
Scuba Dive the Great Barrier Reef
The world's largest coral system is bleaching and changing. Go now, while it's still what it was. Open water certification takes a weekend.
08
White Water Raft Class V Rapids
The Gauley River, the Zambezi, the Futaleufú. Class V is the edge of navigable. Guides will take you there safely.
09
Swim With Whale Sharks
The Maldives, Cancún, Western Australia. These gentle giants are the largest fish on earth. Swimming alongside one is humbling in the best way.
10
Sail Across an Ocean
The Atlantic crossing takes about 3 weeks. Crew positions are available on sailing vessels if you don't own a boat. Days with no land in any direction changes you.
11
Kayak With Orcas in British Columbia
Johnstone Strait during orca season. Paddling alongside killer whales in open water is a contact with wildness you won't forget.
12
Freedive (No Tank, Just Breath)
Freediving courses teach you to descend on a single breath. The silence at 20 meters is unlike anything with scuba gear.
On Foot
The Long Walk
13
Trek to Everest Base Camp
17 days. 5,364 meters. No technical climbing required. The most iconic trek on earth is achievable with moderate fitness and a good guide.
14
Walk the Camino de Santiago
500 miles across northern Spain. You will cry. You will be changed. Thousands of people walk it every year — many have called it the best thing they've ever done.
15
Hike Patagonia's W Trek
Torres del Paine, Chile. Five days through some of the most dramatic landscape on the planet. Wind, mountains, turquoise glacial lakes.
16
Summit a Mountain Above 14,000 Feet
Colorado has 58 peaks over 14,000 feet. Kilimanjaro reaches 19,341. Pick your level. The view from the top earns every step.
17
Complete a Thru-Hike
The Appalachian Trail (2,190 miles), the Pacific Crest Trail, the Continental Divide. Months of walking. The kind of clarity that only distance creates.
18
Trek Through the Amazon
Peru or Brazil, with a proper guide. Three days in the Amazon changes how you understand the word "wilderness."

On preparation: Most people who fail to complete adventure bucket list items don't lack courage — they lack preparation. Every item on this list has a clear training path. The starting point is deciding you're serious.

On Wheels & Speed
Move Fast
19
Drive a Supercar on a Racetrack
Nürburgring in Germany, Silverstone in England. One day driving experiences let you find out what 150mph actually feels like.
20
Motorcycle Across a Continent
Route 66, the Pan-American Highway, Southeast Asia. Long-form motorcycle travel is the most intimate way to see a landscape.
21
Dog Sled in the Arctic
Northern Norway, Alaska, or Canada. Running a dog sled team across frozen tundra in silence is one of the most singular experiences available to humans.
22
Mountain Bike a World-Class Trail
Whistler Bike Park, Moab's Slickrock, the Swiss Alps trails. Technical descents that demand your full attention. That's the point.
Cold & Ice
Go Where It's Frozen
23
See the Northern Lights From Wilderness
Not from a hotel. From a frozen lake, a dog sled, or a snowfield. Aurora borealis at -20°C with no light pollution is something else entirely.
24
Ice Climb a Frozen Waterfall
Banff, Colorado, Norway. A one-day intro course teaches you to ascend vertical ice with crampons and ice axes. Harder than it looks. Worth every attempt.
25
Ski a Black Diamond Run in the Alps
Chamonix, Verbier, Zermatt. European ski terrain is in a different category. If you ski, go once to where the mountains are serious.
26
Spend a Night in an Ice Hotel
Sweden's ICEHOTEL, Canada's Hôtel de Glace. Everything is carved from ice and snow. Sleeping bags rated for -40°C. Genuinely unlike anything else.
27
Explore Antarctica
Expedition cruises reach the Antarctic Peninsula from Ushuaia, Argentina. Standing on the seventh continent with penguins at your feet is a contact with the planet's extremes that stays with you forever.
28
Snowshoe Into a Winter Wilderness
Yellowstone in February, the Norwegian highlands, the Canadian Rockies. The silence of a snowfield at sunrise is one of the most peaceful experiences available.
Wild Encounters
The Natural World at Its Rawest
29
Track Mountain Gorillas in Uganda or Rwanda
One hour in the presence of a gorilla family. Permits cost $1,500. Every person who has done it says it's the most moving wildlife experience of their life.
30
Watch the Wildebeest Migration
1.5 million animals crossing the Mara River. The Serengeti and Masai Mara, July through October. Scale unlike anything in the natural world.
31
Dive With Great White Sharks
Cage diving from Gansbaai, South Africa. You are separated from the ocean's apex predator by a few inches of steel. That proximity recalibrates everything.
32
Watch Sea Turtles Nest at Night
Costa Rica, Greece, Queensland. Sitting in darkness while a sea turtle comes ashore to lay eggs is a privilege that few people have experienced.
