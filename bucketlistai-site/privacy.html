Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need. We never sell your data to anyone. We use your email only to send you things you asked for. You can ask us to delete everything at any time. That's it.

Who We Are

The Bucket List AI is operated at thebucketlistai.com. We help people build personalized bucket lists using AI. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at [email protected].

What Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our service.

Information you provide directly:

  • Your name and location (entered during the quiz — used only to personalize your bucket list)
  • Your answers to quiz questions (what matters to you, your life stage, categories of interest)
  • Your email address (if you choose to save your list or subscribe to our newsletter)

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic analytics data such as pages visited, time on site, and general location (country/region level only)
  • Browser type and device type for technical compatibility purposes

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your personalized bucket list using our AI
  • To send you your saved list if you request it by email
  • To send our newsletter if you subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand how people use our site so we can improve it
  • To respond to any messages or support requests you send us

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, automated decision-making that affects you legally, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

How We Store Your Data

Your bucket list is stored in your browser's local storage by default — meaning it stays on your device and we don't have access to it unless you choose to share it with us via email capture.

If you provide your email address, it is stored securely with our email service provider (MailerLite). We do not store email addresses on our own servers.

Quiz answers used to generate your list are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to produce your personalized results. Anthropic's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Ever.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • MailerLite — our email service provider, used to send newsletters and saved lists
  • Anthropic — your quiz answers are sent to generate your AI bucket list
  • Netlify — our hosting provider, which processes web traffic
  • Unsplash — images for your bucket list items are fetched from their API
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, or court order

All third-party providers we use are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking. We may use analytics tools (such as visitor tracking) to understand aggregate usage patterns — how many people visit, which pages are popular, and where visitors come from geographically. This data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify you personally.

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

Affiliate Links

Some links on our site — particularly "Book This Dream" buttons — are affiliate links to Viator and Booking.com. If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no cost to you. These links do not affect the experiences we recommend. We include affiliate links only to experiences we genuinely believe are worth having.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or update your data
  • Request that we delete your data entirely
  • Unsubscribe from our emails at any time (every email includes an unsubscribe link)
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will notify subscribers of material changes by email.
